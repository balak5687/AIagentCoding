# 🏠 Product HQ

## Mission Statement:

To help garages transition from manual operations to intelligent digital workflows that improve efficiency, visibility, profitability, and customer trust.

### Problem Statement:

Most independent garages and workshops still rely on manual processes, paper job cards, spreadsheets, phone calls, and fragmented communication methods.

This creates operational inefficiencies such as:

- poor job tracking
- delayed customer updates
- inventory mismatch
- billing errors
- lack of business visibility
- inconsistent service workflows

Existing enterprise systems are often expensive, complex, or not tailored for small and medium-sized workshops.

There is a need for a simple, scalable, and intelligent platform designed specifically for modern garage operations.

---

### Core Objectives

- Centralize all garage operations in one platform
- Simplify workshop workflows
- Improve job and service tracking
- Reduce operational delays and manual work
- Provide real-time business visibility
- Improve customer communication and transparency
- Enable data-driven decision making
- Introduce AI-assisted operational support

### Target Users

Business Types:

- Independent garages
- Multi-branch workshops
- Automotive service centers

Primary Users:

- Garage owners
- Service advisors
- Mechanics
- Inventory managers

#### 🔷 Core Platform Modules

The platform is built around nine core operational modules that collectively support customer service operations, inventory management, procurement, workforce coordination, financial tracking, and business visibility.

| # | Module | Purpose |
| --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | **Dashboard** | Real-time operational visibility and KPI monitoring |
| 2 | **Customer Management** | Manage customer profiles and communication |
| 3 | **Vehicle History & Records** | Track vehicle information and service history |
| 4 | **Job Cards & Service Workflow** | Create, assign, and track repair and service jobs |
| 5 | **Inventory & Spare Parts Management** | Manage stock levels, parts usage, and inventory visibility |
| 6 | **Billing & Invoicing** | Generate invoices and track customer payments |
| 7 | **Supplier & Procurement Management** | Manage suppliers, purchase orders, and deliveries |
| 8 | **Mechanic Assignment & Workforce Management** | Track technician workload and workforce operations |
| 9 | **Reports & Analytics** | Provide operational insights and business reporting |
